Access the below-mentioned location.

HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Policies\System

Here select the System entry and then right-click on the white space in the right panel and select New > DWORD (32-bit) Value.

Name this new entry “verbosestatus” and double-click on it and change its value to 1.

Now your PC will tell you exactly what processes it is starting or closing when you start or shut down the PC.
